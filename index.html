About 

I study sculpture in Sint-Lucas in Ghent/ Belgium, last semester I went to Helsinki/Finland for an exchange period for four months. I actually worked mainly in the printmaking department the whole time. 

There I learned a lot, especially about woodcuts. They also introduced artists whose work is not according to the classic rules of printmaking, they showed us work that was innovatory and experimental. They showed us the possibilities of the materials and that we don't have to think in the box. That was when I realised that prints can also be sculptural. 

Now back in Ghent I'm working further with the formality off the prints I made in Helsinki. And these where again developed from an previous sculpture.

I see my works as one big project, a research in form, theme and materiality. My inspirations are architecture, minimalistic forms and Dailey life
